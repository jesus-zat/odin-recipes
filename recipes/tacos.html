Ground beef tacos

Is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a corn or flour tortilla filled with various ingredients like seasoned meat, vegetables, and cheese, then folded around the filling to be eaten by hand

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Ground beef
  • Tomatoo sauce
  • Shredded lettuce
  • White oninon
  • Fresh cilantro
  • lemon

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef: a large skillet over medium-high heat. If using a very lean beef, add a tablespoon of oil. Add the diced onion (if using) and cook until softened, about 4 minutes
  2. Season and simmer:Drain excess fat if necessary. Add taco seasoning (store-bought or homemade) and a splash of water. Stir well to coat the meat evenly. Let it simmer for 3â€“5 minutes until the mixture thickens and the flavors combine
  3. shells and topings: the tortillas in a dry skillet, microwave, or oven until soft and pliable. Meanwhile, prep toppings such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, onion, cilantro, salsa, sour cream, or lime wedges
  4. Assemble and serve:Spoon the seasoned beef into each warm tortilla. Add your preferred toppings, drizzle with sauce or lime, and serve immediately while hot
