Beef tostadas

Beef tostadas are a classic Mexican dish made with crispy tortilla shells topped with refried beans, seasoned ground beef, and fresh toppings like lettuce, tomato, cheese, and crema. They're crunchy, flavorful, and perfect for a quick, satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Taco seasoning or spices (cumin, chili powder, garlic powder)
  • 1-2 tablespoons oil (if needed)
  • Tostada shells (store-bought or baked tortillas)
  • Crema or sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Sliced avocado or guacamole

Steps

  1. Cook the beef:Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add a splash of oil if needed, then add the ground beef. Cook, breaking it apart with a spoon, until browned and no longer pink. Add diced onion and cook until softened, about 3â€“4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Season and simmer:Season and simmer:Add taco seasoning or your preferred spices (cumin, garlic powder, chili powder). Pour in a small splash of water and stir to coat the meat evenly. Let it simmer for 3-5 minutes until the mixture thickens and becomes flavorful.
  3. Prepare tostadas and toppings:Warm store-bought tostada shells or bake tortillas until crispy. Meanwhile, prepare toppings such as shredded lettuce, refried beans, diced tomatoes, crema or sour cream, cheese, sliced avocado, and salsa.
  4. Assemble and serve: Spread a layer of refried beans on each tostada shell. Add a generous spoonful of the seasoned beef, then top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, crema, avocado, and salsa. Serve immediately to keep the tostadas crisp.
